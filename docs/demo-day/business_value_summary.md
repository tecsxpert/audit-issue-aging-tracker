# Business Value Summary

## What The AI Does

- Explains aged audit issues in clear language.
- Recommends practical remediation steps.
- Generates structured audit reports.
- Helps teams prioritize high-risk issues.
- Supports repeatable closure evidence.

## Why It Is Useful

Manual audit review is slow when issues pile up across systems, owners, and risk levels. The AI service reduces the time needed to understand each issue and gives teams a consistent starting point for action.

## Business Value

- Faster audit triage.
- Better remediation prioritization.
- More consistent reporting.
- Reduced dependency on manual write-ups.
- Stronger evidence for audit closure.
- Clearer communication between security, compliance, engineering, and leadership.

## Security Value

- JWT-protected AI endpoints.
- Prompt injection blocking.
- PII rejection before external AI calls.
- Rate limiting to reduce abuse.
- Structured failure responses during provider outages.

## Efficiency Improvement

The AI service does not replace auditors or engineers. It reduces repetitive analysis work so reviewers can focus on decisions, ownership, and closure quality.
